Slide Display Options

Background Image

Here you can upload a custom background image to appear in the Slide.

YouTube or Vimeo Video

Enter the YouTube or Vimeo URL in the provided field. Do not enter the embed code. If a video URL is entered here it will overwrite the Featured Image.

Slide Layout

Choose a layout for the Slide. Left aligns the Slide title left and Right will aligns the Slide title right. If you choose a Left or Right layout the Featured Image will appear on the opposite side of the title. Center aligns the title in the center and will not display a Featured Image.

Slide Transition

Choose a transition for your slide.

Slide Link URL

Enter an optional link URL for the Slide. Once the URL is entered in this field the entire Slide becomes a link. Note: This will overwrite a Call To Action button.
