Portfolio Options

Optional Subtitle

The subtitle appears below the title of the page. It defaults to the theme's highlight color, but you can change it for all Portfolio Items on the Portfolio Page, under Page Options > Page Subtitle Color.

Optional Content Area Intro

If you choose to enter one, the intro will appear before any entered main content. It appears bold and at larger font size than the regular content.

YouTube or Vimeo Video

Here you can enter a Youtube or Vimeo URL. Note this will overwrite the featured Image, but you may still wish to have a Featured Image as it will appear as the thumbnail on your main Portolio Page
